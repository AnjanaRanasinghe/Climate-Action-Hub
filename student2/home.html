Our Mission:

Our mission is to mobilize global action towards mitigating climate change, safeguarding the planet's ecosystems, and fostering sustainable development for present and future generations. Through collaboration, innovation, and advocacy, we strive to address the urgent challenges posed by climate change and create a more resilient and equitable world for all.

About:

Here, we aim to illuminate the vital efforts undertaken by the UN to address one of the most pressing challenges of our time: climate change. With a focus on sustainability, resilience, and international cooperation, the United Nations plays a pivotal role in driving global initiatives to mitigate greenhouse gas emissions, adapt to changing environmental conditions, and foster a more sustainable future for generations to come. Through innovative policies, scientific research, and collaborative partnerships, we strive to raise awareness, catalyze action, and inspire meaningful change at every level of society.

Join us on this journey as we explore the complexities of climate change and empower ourselves to make a positive difference. Together, we can pave the way towards a more resilient, equitable, and sustainable world.

Projects

Green Climate Fund
(GCF)

  • The Green Climate Fund was established as a mechanism to assist developing countries in their efforts to respond to the challenges of climate change. It aims to channel financial resources to low-emission and climate-resilient projects and programs.

  • The GCF operates through a network of partnerships with governments, private sector entities, civil society organizations, and international financial institutions. It provides funding for projects and programs that promote sustainable development and climate resilience in developing countries. The fund supports a wide range of activities, including renewable energy projects, adaptation measures, and capacity-building initiatives.

Nationally Determined Contributions (NDCs)

  • Nationally Determined Contributions are commitments made by countries to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and adapt to climate change impacts under the Paris Agreement. Each country sets its own NDCs based on its national circumstances, capabilities, and priorities.

  • The UN Climate Action supports countries in enhancing their NDCs by providing technical assistance, capacity-building, and facilitating dialogue and cooperation among nations. This involves helping countries to set ambitious yet achievable targets, develop robust plans and policies, mobilize financial resources, and implement measures to track progress towards their climate goals.

Read More...

Climate Technology Centre and Network (CTCN)

  • The Climate Technology Centre and Network is a mechanism established under the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC) to facilitate the transfer of environmentally sound technologies for climate change mitigation and adaptation in developing countries.

  • The CTCN assists developing countries in accessing and deploying appropriate climate technologies by providing technical assistance, knowledge sharing, and capacity-building support. It operates through a network of regional and sectoral experts who offer expertise in various areas such as renewable energy, energy efficiency, sustainable agriculture, and climate-resilient infrastructure. The CTCN helps countries identify technology needs, assess available options, and develop tailored solutions to address their climate challenges.
